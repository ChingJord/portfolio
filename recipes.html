Kale and Rock Melon Salad

  • 2 leaves of kale
  • 1/4 rock melon
  • 100g roughly chopped walnuts
  • 30g sheep feta
  • 4 large mint leaves
  • Extra virgin olive oil

Wash and roughly chop the kale and place in a bowl.
Deseed and skin the melon then finely slice and add to the bowl.
Julienne the mint leaves, add to the bowl and mix thoroughly.
Once plated sprinkle with walnuts and crumble over feta.
Drizzle with olive oil and enjoy.

Summer Salmon

  • 500 grams salmon
  • 3cm knob of ginger (peeled)
  • 2 clove of garlic
  • 1 tablespoon extra virgin olive oil
  • Juice of half a lemon
  • Zest of a lemon
  • Salt & Pepper

Debone the salmon but leave skin on.
Fine dice garlic and ginger.
Drizzle olive oil over the flesh side of the salmon and rub in the garlic and ginger.
Sprinkle over the lemon zest.
Salt and pepper to taste.
Fry flesh side down on a medium heat for 1 minute then flip and and fry on a high heat until the skin is crispy.
Sprinkle with the juice of half a lemon and serve with a lemon wedge.
